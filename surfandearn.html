Surf & Earn — External Options

You can earn rewards or crypto simply by browsing the web using third-party tools. These do not affect your participation in LLA5785 and can be used independently of our platform.

Here are a few popular options:
  • Brave Browser — Earn BAT tokens by enabling ads while browsing.
  • Presearch — Earn PRE tokens for searching the web.
  • CoinGecko Rewards — Collect candy points for daily visits.
  • StormGain — Mine crypto passively in your browser (requires registration).

You’re free to use these services if you wish to earn extra from your daily browsing — completely outside of LLA5785.

Note: LLA5785 does not promote cryptocurrency earning programs. The above list is for informational purposes only and you use them at your own discretion.
